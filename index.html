About the Project

This website presents the results of the Eagle Ford group project completed during my time at the UT Data&Visualization Boot Camp. The purpose of this project was to make a foray into oil&gas industry and identify key metrics for investment decisions.

We approach the problem from the standpoint of a burgeoning exploration company seeking funding for an Eagle Ford Shale asset. Investing in a quality area is our primary concern. We define a quality area as an area that is primarily oil and has high production values. Costs are important and are prohibitive at exorbitant levels. We performed an analysis of sub-Plays to define areas (sub-plays) of biggest opportunity. Furthermore, sub-plays of high well and operator activity are not only indicative of geologically sound areas, but also have more data on drilling and completion costs and methods.
