Average Estimated Recovery By Sub-Play

This chart visualizes estimated ultimate recovery (EUR) for each sub-play in Eagle Ford. (EUR) is a production term commonly used in the oil and gas industry and is a metric used for approximation of the quantity of oil or gas that is potentially recoverable or has already been recovered from a reserve or well. It is of the utmost importance that drilling projects meet an acceptable EUR threshold for a project to be considered viable and profitable. Here we see that Southeast Gas, Southwest Gas, and Maverisck Condensate, that have the lowest well count (see Activity by Sub-play), have a higher average BOE production. This, in turn, is indicative of a bigger production potential in these sub-plays. However, further analysis is required for a more accurate valuation of the potential oil reserves.
